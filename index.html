The Greater Library of Alexandria

"... one of the largest and most significant libraries of the modern world."

The Greater Library of Alexandria is an archive of historically significant online fiction. Each document contained within has been personally edited and reformatted to be e-book compatible, whilst carefully preserving the original text inscribed by the author. For the library of Alexandria during the reign of Ptolemy II Philadelphus, see Library of Alexandria.
